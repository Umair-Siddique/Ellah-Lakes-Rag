The company is a public quoted company incorporated on 22 August, 1980 as a limited liability company. 
It was converted to a public limited company on 16 June, 1992. It engages in the business of palm oil plantations and cassava farming.

In May 2019, Ellah Lakes acquired Telluria Farms, an oil production company with 2400 hectares of farm land in Edo State. 
This changed the corporate mission to the production of palm oil and its derivatives including cassava and other cash crops.

The company also acquired 500 hectares of land in Ondo State to be used for planting cassava, soya and maize.

A subsidiary company — ELP Sunshine Limited — was incorporated into which Ellah Lakes holds 80%.

Adani Staple Crop Processing Zone Food Company Limited was incorporated into which the Company has a subsidiary: Adani Staple Crop Processing Zone Food Company Limited.
